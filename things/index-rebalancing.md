# Index rebalancing

Index rebalancing is the scheduled update of a market index. The provider checks eligibility, adds or deletes members, and resets weights.

The pieces connect like this. The [[index-methodology]] sets the rules. [[market-cap]] and [[free-float]] feed those rules; together they drive which securities qualify and how much weight each gets. On the rebalance date, applying the rules to fresh data produces a set of [[additions-and-deletions]].

Rebalance dates are published in advance.
